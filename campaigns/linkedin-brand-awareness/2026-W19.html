Firm-level completion numbers from LinkedIn appear inflated. Several firms show completion-of-viewers rates above 50%, which is implausible for a 45-second video. We're treating this table as relative engagement signal — useful for ranking firms by interest level — not as exact counts. Likely causes:

  • LinkedIn double-counts completions on autoplay refreshes when the same user is served the video multiple times
  • Attribution drift — completions can be aggregated against firm of record at impression time, stacking the same firm across multiple user sessions
  • Possible non-human or auto-replay traffic in specific firm clusters
  • One or more of the four active ad sets may have a delivery quirk producing inflated counts

Flagged with Danila for validation before W20. Until then, use this table to identify firms showing relative engagement, not as a literal headcount of who watched what.
